Dark Souls is a game series known for its crushing difficulty. The series has the player crawling through its levels which are designed as dark dungeons and ruined cities, to fit with the name of the game. There are currently 3 entries in the series, each building on the concepts of the previous game. This series has inspired a generation of games and remains one of the greatest challenges and most referenced games among all gamers. Please take a moment to look through the site to learn a little about each game and the developer!

New Title Announced

From Software, developer of Dark Souls, has announced their next title "Elden Ring" which will be a spiritual successor to the Dark Souls games. The story and lore of the game are being developed in colaboration with George R. R. Martin.
